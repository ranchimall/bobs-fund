Bob's Fund
on FLO Blockchain

Bob's Fund is a 20 year long term Bitcoin price linked product. Investors are entitled to 100 percent of Bitcoin price gains, but they must hold for 20 years. Over a very long time period, investor returns on an asset like Bitcoin should outstrip returns on conventional assets like real estate and stocks. The management fees on this product is zero. RanchiMall earns by having invested an equal amount as every investor, thus the interests of fund manager, and fund investors are totally aligned.
